What is Unclaimed Property?

States hold funds when owners can’t be reached or payments aren’t deposited. Companies often miss these because mail, vendors, or records changed over time.

Common sources Vendor credits, refunds, deposits, checks, account balances.
Why it happens Address changes, mergers, payroll/vendor updates, accounting transitions.
What we do Identify likely matches and guide the recovery steps with documentation.

Why businesses don’t know this money exists

Most missed funds are the result of normal business changes — not wrongdoing.

Mail and address changes

Checks and notices go to old addresses and never reach the right team.

Vendor / processor transitions

Refunds, credits, and balances can be left behind during system changes.

Mergers, DBAs, old entity names

Funds can be listed under prior names or legacy records.
